Hi, my name is Pedro Santos and I am a visual communications designer.

As a visual person, the necessity to perfect everything simply turned into a must do. It all started with a simple average critique of visual content found on our daily basis such as posters and banners. As the days went by, I started questioning if Graphic Design could turn into my dream career and so it did.

As the Graphic Design Program at Algonquin College comes to an end, my portfolio grew with a diverse content of master works tailored to perfect the client’s vision and giving me the satisfaction to remember the reason why it all started and the necessity to perfect everything I create. From branding to layouts and advertisement, I am confident that my services will rival any visual design content on current days standards and give my client’s the 100% satisfaction they always deserve.
